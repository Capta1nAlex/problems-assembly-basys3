Program a countdown device for the microwave that will wait the desired value after start/reset and then start a countdown that will end at 0. 
Requirements
Minutes and seconds will be displayed on the display (both values ​​will be displayed on 2 digits in the decimal system)
after the end of the countdown, a blinking text* will be displayed with information about the end of the countdown (eg: EGGS ARE DONE).
The microprocessor is clocked at a frequency of 16 MHz. In order to make solution accurate, the time of output execution should be considered as well.

*The text should disappear once a second and reappear the next second
